Hernando Lozano

Civil engineer with 45+ years of experience in design and calculus of projects for public and private sector customers in the petrochemical, industrial, electric and oil industries. Projects include large scale bridges, buildings, coastal platforms and industrial facilities in Venezuela, Colombia, Aruba, Curacao and Panama. University professor for over 30 years in the courses of “Concrete” and “Structural Systems” at the Universidad del Zulia (Zulia State University) in Venezuela. He has successfully coordinated a wide variety of cross-functional and international teams. Since the beginning of his career, he has led several engineering projects, and has simultaneously developed an academic career specializing in structures and bridges.
